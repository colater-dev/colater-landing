# Colater - Foundations

## What Is Colater?
An AI-accelerated design agency that works with startups. World-class human designers + specialised AI agents = high quality work, faster, tighter iteration loops.

## Primary CTA
Book a call/meeting (with optional fields for more info about their project)

## Services Offered
- **Brand Identity** - Logos, brand guidelines, visual identity systems, naming
- **Product/UI Design** - App/web UI design, design systems, prototyping
- **Web Design & Dev** - Marketing sites, landing pages, Webflow/Framer builds
- **Pitch Decks & Collateral** - Investor decks, sales materials, one-pagers, social assets

## Tone
Warm & approachable - Friendly, human, inviting. Think Notion or Loom.
